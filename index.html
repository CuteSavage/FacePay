Our Main Features

Secure Payment

When you make a purchase, FacePay doesn't send your account information, it only transfers your Facial-Digital-Print already saved on our servers, and even this data is encrypted using our military grade technology.

Futuristic Technology

The facial recognition technology used by FacePay allows your face to be identified by your phone's camera or the image sensor on the LCD screen attached to the cashier's counter, allowing you to perform your transaction in less than 5 seconds, and in some cases without requiring any cellular data from your device.

Contactless Payment

FacePay works by recognizing your face through the device's camera, helping you avoid touching buttons and terminals, handling cards, and exchanging cash. So you keep your hands clean and are less likely to pick up — and spread — germs.

FacePay is safer than using a plastic credit, debit, or prepaid card. Every transaction requires you to authenticate with your face ID. Your card number and identity aren’t shared with the merchant, and your actual card numbers aren’t stored on your device or on our servers. When you pay in stores, neither FacePay nor your device sends your actual card numbers to merchants. When you pay within apps and on the web, the merchant will only receive information like name, email address, billing and shipping addresses that you authorize to share to fulfill your order. FacePay retains anonymous transaction information such as approximate purchase amount. This information can’t be tied back to you and never includes what you're buying. Apps that use FacePay must have a privacy policy that you can view, which governs the use of your data.
